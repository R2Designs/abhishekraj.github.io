Your List

Understanding our users we noticed a sticky behaviour where everytime they open their shop and order products for their shop, they go through all the app present in the market and create a list of products he wants to buy and compare prices to find the cheaper products. We wanted to make the same implementation for app where a user can visit the app everyday and we create a list of products that only he buys or has whitelisted, and provide a single platform to check the prices, add your shops skus and buy it all together. this helped us increase productivity by 20%, session conversion increased by 4%, 7.5% WAU, and a screen conversion of 21.5%
