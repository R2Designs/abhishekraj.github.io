Sign up (Lead generation)

In 2019, HSL introduced city bikes in Vantaa. A completely new dockless city bike system meant that we had to rethink the whole site structure from scratch, as there now was two different bike systems under the same HSL brand.

The new website offers an effortless way to buy daily, weekly and season passes for both bike systems. Users can also view their personal details, trip history and update payment details from the site.

casebycase (smallcase Publisher)

DigiTransit is an open source journey planning solution created by HSL and the Finnish Transport Agency. HSL journey planner (Reittiopas), which is based on DigiTransit, is being used by hundreds of thousands of commuters and travelers every day.

During my time at HSL, I was part of the multi-vendor team responsible of the journey planner development. Working closely with the product owner and developers, we introduced features like displaying disruption info across the whole application, live view, favorites, new zones and 3rd party mobility services like scooters and ferries.
